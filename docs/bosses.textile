h1. Bosses

Each dungeon contains a boss character. The player can make their choice on which boss they would like to face. That boss determines the dungeon, encounter types and layout of the floors.
Bosses determine what the player's experience will be. They have a few factors that play into what the player will experience in that dungeon.

h2. Determinations

Bosses determine the dungeon composition

- Encounter Types := bosses may specify which encounter types are found in this dungeon, or which are more common to occurr. Some bosses may specialize in traps for example.
- Layout := bosses may specify that the player must clear 4 floors before facing the boss.
- Room count := bosses may also specify how many rooms there are and on what floors.
