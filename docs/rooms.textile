h1. Rooms

Rooms are the individual entities on a floor. A room is comprised of an event. That event could be one of the following:

* Treasure
* Encounter Creature
* Encounter Trap
* Encounter Puzzle
* Spa (Healing)
* Shop

h2. Rewards

Rooms contain a reward. Depending on the type of room, there may be some objectives required to complete before recieving the reward. Most rooms offer several rewards:

- Gold := a currency used to buy items or services.
- Stat := a stat increase on your character.
- Skill := a new skill the player can use.
- Effect := a status or effect on the player.
